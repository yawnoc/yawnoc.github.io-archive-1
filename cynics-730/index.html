A cynic's 7.30 (and other programs)

First created: 2019-08-30
Date modified: 2020-03-27

Originally the plan was to make comic strips with proper speech and thought bubbles, but I don't think I can pull that off with just HTML and CSS. So if you see {highlighted and curly-bracketed text in Comic Neue}, it's supposed to be in a thought bubble.

You may not care about politics, but politics cares about you. And by that I mean: politics is out to screw you.

2020

  • 26 March 2020 Deputy Chief Medical Officer Paul Kelly on the fight to contain coronavirus (better entitled "Professor Paul Kelly speaking politician-speak")
  • 12 March 2020 Josh Frydenberg says the Government's surplus prediction was based on 'forecasts at the time'
  • 3 March 2020 Scott Morrison says economic plan for coronavirus to be revealed before May budget
  • 2 March 2020 Energy Minister Angus Taylor discusses carbon emissions and Clover Moore

2019

  • 14 October 2019 Energy Minister Angus Taylor discusses Snowy Hydro 2.0
  • 10 September 2019 (The Bolt Report, not 7.30) Gladys Liu discusses membership of various Chinese Associations
  • 3 September 2019 Anthony Albanese discusses the NSW Labor donation scandal
  • 28 August 2019 Education Minister Dan Tehan discusses NAPLAN and the latest results
