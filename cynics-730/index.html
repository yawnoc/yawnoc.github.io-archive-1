A cynic's 7.30 (and other programs)

First created: 20190830
Last modified: 20200316

Originally the plan was to make comic strips with proper speech and thought bubbles, but I don't think I can pull that off with just HTML and CSS. So if you see {highlighted and curly-bracketed text in Comic Neue}, it's supposed to be in a thought bubble.

You may not care about politics, but politics cares about you. And by that I mean: politics is out to screw you.
